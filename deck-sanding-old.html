Complete Deck Sanding Solutions

Our professional deck sanding services restore weathered, grey, and damaged timber decking to its original beauty. Using industrial-grade sanders and quality finishes, we transform old decks into stunning outdoor spaces.

Surface Preparation

Complete cleaning, nail setting, and surface preparation before sanding begins. We ensure your deck is ready for the restoration process.

Professional Sanding

Multi-grit sanding process removes old finishes, stains, and weathering. We use professional equipment for consistent, smooth results.

Finishing Options

Oil, stain, or paint finishes to protect and enhance your deck's appearance. Choose from various colors and protection levels.
