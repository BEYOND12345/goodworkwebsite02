Bangalow Heritage Property Specialist

Preserving Bangalow's historic homes and modern hinterland properties with traditional craftsmanship and modern techniques

Bangalow's unique position in the Northern Rivers hinterland creates specific challenges for property maintenance. Higher rainfall, native timber construction, and heritage building requirements demand specialized knowledge and appropriate materials.

Heritage Home Specialist: I work extensively with Bangalow's historic properties, understanding council heritage requirements, traditional joinery techniques, and period-appropriate materials. From Byron Street's iconic Queenslanders to rural heritage cottages, I preserve character while improving functionality.

Hinterland Climate Expertise: The elevated location means different weather patterns - more rain, morning mists, and temperature variations that affect timber and paint. I select materials and techniques suited to Bangalow's specific microclimate.

Heritage-Listed Property Specialist

Bangalow's rich timber industry history left us with beautiful heritage homes throughout Byron Street, Station Street, and surrounding areas. These properties require specialized maintenance that respects their historical significance.

Heritage Services I Provide:

  • Traditional timber joinery restoration using period-appropriate techniques
  • VJ wall repairs matching original profiles and timber species
  • Heritage window restoration - sash windows, casements, and shutters
  • Council-compliant modifications that preserve character
  • Lead paint removal following WorkSafe protocols
  • Termite damage repairs using traditional framing methods

Working closely with Byron Shire Council's heritage requirements, I ensure all work maintains the historical integrity while improving livability and meeting modern building standards.
