Lennox Head Beachfront Expertise

Located on Seven Mile Beach between Byron Bay and Ballina, Lennox Head properties are constantly exposed to salt air, sand, and coastal weather patterns. From the iconic surf break to the peaceful Skennars Head, every property here needs specialized coastal maintenance.

We understand the unique challenges of beachfront living - from salt corrosion on metal fixtures to sand infiltration in moving parts. Our maintenance approach uses marine-grade materials and protective treatments specifically designed for this harsh but beautiful coastal environment.

Whether it's a weekend surf shack, a permanent beachfront residence, or a holiday rental property, we provide maintenance that preserves your investment while embracing the relaxed coastal lifestyle that makes Lennox Head special.
