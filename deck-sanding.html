Professional Deck Sanding Services - Byron Bay & Northern Rivers

I understand what Northern Rivers weather does to timber decks. Sun, rain, and coastal conditions leave decks grey, splintered, and weathered. I properly restore them using professional-grade sanders and proven techniques that actually last in our coastal climate.

I don't rush the sanding process. Multi-grit progression removes old finishes and weathering while preserving the timber's integrity. I use quality stains and oils that penetrate properly - not cheap products that peel off in 12 months. The result is a deck that looks better than new and handles coastal conditions.

Professional Sanding

I use multi-grit progression (40-80-120-220) that removes weathering while preserving timber integrity, not quick fixes that damage the wood.

Coastal Expertise

15+ years specialising in Byron Bay decks means I understand salt air damage and use marine-grade products that actually last.

Quality Finishes

I apply proven stains and oils from Cabot's and Feast Watson that penetrate properly, not cheap coatings that peel off.

Structural Assessment

I check for loose boards, protruding nails, and structural issues before sanding, ensuring a safe and complete restoration.

Proper Preparation

I protect plants, clean properly, and mask adjacent surfaces - professional preparation for professional results.

Weather Timing

I schedule work for optimal weather conditions, ensuring proper curing and finish adhesion in our coastal environment.
