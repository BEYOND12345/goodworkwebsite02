This Is A Deck Emergency When:

Structural Collapse or Boards Giving Way

Deck boards sagging, cracking, or breaking when walked on. Immediate safety risk to anyone using the deck.

Airbnb Guests Arriving Tomorrow

Guests checking in and the deck is unusable. Your review score and booking income are at risk.

Party or Event in 24-48 Hours

Wedding, birthday party, family gathering planned and your deck entertainment area is broken.

Storm Damage Requiring Securing

Recent storm damaged deck structure, loose boards flapping, or railings that could fall and cause injury.

Property Inspection Next Day

Building inspection, insurance assessment, or property sale inspection where deck issues will be noted.

Railing Completely Loose

Balustrade or railing that's no longer secure, creating serious safety hazard especially with children.
