How much does flooring installation cost in Byron Bay?

What flooring is best for Byron Bay's coastal climate?

For Byron Bay's humid coastal conditions, hybrid flooring and luxury vinyl are excellent choices due to water resistance. Quality laminate with moisture barriers also works well. Hardwood flooring needs proper sealing and climate acclimatization.

How long does flooring installation take?

Most single rooms take 1-2 days for completion. Whole house installations typically take 3-7 days depending on size and material. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your property.

Do you handle subfloor preparation?

Yes, professional subfloor preparation is included in all projects. This includes leveling, moisture testing, barrier installation, and ensuring proper foundation for lasting results in coastal conditions.
