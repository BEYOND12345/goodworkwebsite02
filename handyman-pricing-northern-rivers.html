Pricing FAQs

Do you charge a callout fee?

No callout fee for standard appointments within my service area. Emergency callouts have a $120 first hour rate which includes travel time.

Are materials included in the price?

Labour and materials are quoted separately for transparency. You'll receive an itemized quote showing exactly what you're paying for.

What's your minimum charge?

2 hour minimum for standard work. Small jobs under 2 hours are still charged at the 2 hour rate to cover travel time and setup.

Do you offer payment plans?

For larger projects over $1,000, I can arrange a deposit and progress payment schedule. Payment is due on completion for smaller jobs.

Is there extra cost for weekends?

Standard rates apply for scheduled weekend work. Emergency callouts on weekends and after hours are charged at the emergency rate.

What if the job takes longer than quoted?

Fixed price quotes won't change unless additional work is discovered and approved. Any changes are discussed before proceeding.

Ready for Your Free Quote?

No obligation quotes provided within 24 hours. Emergency repairs available same day.
