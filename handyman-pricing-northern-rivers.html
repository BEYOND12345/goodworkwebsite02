Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge?

My standard rate is $65/hour with a 2-hour minimum ($130). I also offer half-day ($250) and full-day ($480) rates. Every job is different though, so I prefer to give you an accurate quote for your specific needs. Quotes are free and there's no obligation. Call for a quote.

Do you charge for quotes?

No, all quotes are completely free with no obligation. I'll usually respond the same day, and for most jobs I can give you a price over the phone or via text.

What about materials?

Materials are charged at cost - I'll show you the receipts. I don't mark up materials, you only pay what I paid plus my labor time.

Is there a minimum charge?

Yes, there's a 2-hour minimum ($130) for most jobs. This covers my travel time and setup. For very quick jobs, I'll often quote a fixed price instead.

Do you offer payment plans?

For larger jobs over $500, I can arrange progress payments. Typically 50% upfront for materials and 50% on completion. Call to discuss.
