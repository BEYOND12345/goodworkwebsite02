Meet Dan, Your Local Northern Rivers Handyman

Treating every property like it's my own. Byron Bay local committed to quality workmanship and honest service.

G'day, I'm Dan

I've been working as a handyman throughout the Northern Rivers region, and I still love what I do. There's something deeply satisfying about fixing things properly – whether it's a kitchen cupboard door that won't close properly or restoring a weathered deck to its former glory.

As a Byron Bay local, I understand the unique challenges this coastal environment presents. The salt air, humidity, and occasional storms mean homes here need extra care and the right materials. I've learned which products work best here and which shortcuts to avoid.

My approach is simple: treat every job like it's in my own home. That means using quality materials, taking the time to do things properly, and being upfront about costs and timelines. No surprises, no rushing, just honest work done well.

When I'm not wielding tools, you'll find me surfing the local breaks or exploring the hinterland trails. This is my home too, and I'm proud to help keep the community's properties in great shape.
