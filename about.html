About Good Hands

I'm Daniel, the person behind Good Hands. I work directly with homeowners and property managers across Byron Bay and the Northern Rivers to keep properties looking and working their best.

What to expect

• You deal with me one on one. No call centres, no crews, just clear communication from start to finish.

• I turn up when I say I will and get the job done properly.

• I bring a craftsman approach to every task. That means measuring twice, finishing carefully, and fixing things so they last.

• I handle urgent repairs, guest ready touch ups, and the detailed carpentry work that other trades often avoid.

Where my craft comes from

I learned my trade from my dad, a master craftsman and carpenter. Watching him taught me the value of precision, patience, and a clean finish. That upbringing influences everything I do. I am proud to bring that old school attention to detail into modern jobs, whether it is a door that needs perfect alignment, a lock that must work flawlessly, or a custom cabinet that needs a precise fit.
