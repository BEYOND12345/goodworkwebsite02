Deck Restoration Services - Byron Bay & Northern Rivers

I see what the Northern Rivers weather does to timber decks. Sun, rain, salt air - it all takes its toll. I properly restore weathered decks using quality materials and proven techniques. Sand back to bare timber, apply quality stain or oil, and protect against future weather damage.

I don't just slap on a quick coat and call it done. I assess the timber condition, repair any structural issues, sand properly through different grits, and apply the right products for your specific timber and exposure. I use quality Cabot's, Feast Watson, and other proven deck products that actually last.

Deck Sanding

I sand back weathered timber to reveal fresh wood underneath

Timber Repair

I replace damaged boards and fix structural issues properly

Staining & Oiling

I apply quality products that protect and enhance your timber

Structural Assessment

I check bearers, joists, and posts for safety issues

Balustrade Restoration

I restore or replace deck railings to match your deck

Waterproofing

I apply protective coatings that keep moisture out
