Questions Everyone Asks About Carpentry

Common questions from Byron Bay to Ballina customers

How much do carpenters charge in Byron Bay?

Traditional carpenters charge Contact for quote/hour or give fixed quotes on bigger jobs. I keep my rates competitive - quality work without the premium quote. For most projects, I'll give you a custom quote based on exactly what you need.

What's the difference between you and a "real" carpenter?

Skills-wise? Nothing for the work I do. I won't frame your house or build roof trusses. But for custom built-ins, decks, pergolas, shelving, furniture? I've got the same skills, just charge less because I don't have a fancy shopfront.

What areas do you cover?

Byron Bay and everywhere within about 60km - Lennox Head, Bangalow, Mullumbimby, Ballina, Brunswick Heads, Ocean Shores, Lismore, Tweed Heads. From Suffolk Park to Wategos, Arts & Industry Estate to Tallow Beach.

How long does custom carpentry take?

Depends on the project. A simple shelf? Same day. Custom built-in wardrobes? Usually 2-3 days. A full deck? Week or two depending on size. I'll give you a realistic timeline when I quote.

Do you have examples of your carpentry work?

Absolutely. I'll show you photos of similar projects when we chat. I can also put you in touch with other Byron Bay customers if you want to hear directly from them.

Do you use sustainable timber?

Where possible and practical, yes. I'll talk you through timber options - what's sustainable, what suits your project, what fits your budget. Some projects need treated pine for outdoor use, others work great with recycled timber.
