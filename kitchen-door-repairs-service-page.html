Kitchen Door Problems We Fix

Professional repairs using quality hardware and materials that last

Kitchen doors that won't close properly are one of the most common - and frustrating - problems in Northern Rivers homes. Salt air, humidity, and daily use take their toll on hinges, drawer sliders, and cabinet hardware.

We fix kitchen door problems properly the first time, using quality Blum hardware and marine-grade materials that withstand our coastal environment. From Byron Bay to Tweed Heads, we understand the specific challenges Northern Rivers kitchens face.

Cupboard Doors Won't Close

Doors that don't close properly or spring back open. Usually caused by worn hinges, warped doors, or misaligned frames. We adjust or replace hinges and realign doors for smooth operation.

Broken Hinges & Hardware

Hinges that have pulled out of the cabinet frame or snapped completely. We install quality Blum soft-close hinges that are designed to last in coastal conditions.

Drawer Sliders Stuck

Drawers that stick, won't open fully, or have fallen off their tracks. We replace worn sliders with heavy-duty options that handle daily use and coastal humidity.

Warped Cabinet Doors

Doors that have warped from humidity and won't sit flush. We can plane doors back to size or recommend replacement if warping is severe.

Loose Door Handles

Handles that wobble or have come loose. We tighten existing hardware or install new handles with proper backing plates for security.

Cabinet Door Hanging

New cabinet doors that need hanging or existing doors that need rehinging. Professional door hanging ensures smooth operation and proper alignment.
