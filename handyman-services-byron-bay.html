Questions People Ask About Handyman Services

Common questions from Byron Bay to Ballina customers

What exactly does a handyman do?

Pretty much anything that doesn't need a licensed electrician, plumber, or structural engineer. General repairs, maintenance, installations, carpentry, painting, deck work, furniture assembly - if you're not sure, just ask.

How much do handymen charge in Byron Bay?

Most charge Local Expert per hour. I keep my rates competitive because I'm building my business. For bigger jobs, I'll give you a fixed quote so there's no surprises.

Do you have a minimum charge?

2-hour minimum for most jobs, but honestly I'm flexible. If it's a 20-minute job and you're local, we can work something out. I'm not going to charge you Contact for quote to hang one picture.

What areas do you cover?

Byron Bay and about 60km radius - Lennox Head, Bangalow, Mullumbimby, Ballina, Brunswick Heads, Ocean Shores, Lismore, Tweed Heads. If you're not sure, just call and ask.

How quickly can you come?

Usually within a week, often sooner. Emergencies I'll prioritize and try to get there same day or next day.

What if I only have a small job?

Perfect! I actually want small jobs. That's my whole thing - doing the work other handymen won't bother with. No job too small.

Yes, we handle everything from small repairs (fixing a door, replacing a hinge) to larger projects (full deck restorations, painting entire homes, custom carpentry). No job too small or too large.

What makes a good handyman service in Byron Bay?

A good Byron Bay handyman understands coastal maintenance (salt air, humidity, marine-grade products), turns up on time, does quality work, communicates clearly, and cleans up afterwards. That's what we deliver.
