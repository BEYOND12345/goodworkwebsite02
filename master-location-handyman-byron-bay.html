How much does handyman work cost in Byron Bay?
I charge $65/hour for most work, with a $150 minimum. Deck sanding is $33/m², painting $16/m², fence installation $33/linear meter. Small jobs: $150-250. Half day projects: $300-600. Materials are separate and I'll always get approval before buying anything.
Do you really service all of Byron Bay?
Yes! From Byron Bay Lighthouse to Suffolk Park, Bangalow to Mullumbimby. I understand the specific challenges of coastal properties - salt air damage, marine environments, and rapid weather changes that affect Byron Bay homes.
How quickly can you start work in Byron Bay?
I aim to start within one week of your inquiry. Emergency repairs can often be done within 24-48 hours. I understand the Byron Bay holiday rental market and can work around booking schedules when needed.

Then I'll fix it. Simple as that. I live and work in this Byron Bay community, so my reputation depends on getting it right. Your satisfaction is my priority.
