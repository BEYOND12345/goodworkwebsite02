A Bit About Me - And Why I'm Different

Dan - Good Hands Handyman

I'm Dan, and I emigrated here from England. I've had a few career changes in my life - I even used to run an animation company - but I always come back to working with my hands because that's what I love.

I was trained by my dad, who's an expert carpenter. I worked for him for years learning the craft properly. No shortcuts, no "she'll be right" attitude - just proper technique and attention to detail.

When I built my own house (and my own tiny home), I had to deal with a lot of service providers. I was really unhappy with the casual attitude and poor attention to detail I kept seeing. That's exactly what I don't want to be.

As a father myself, I understand the pressure you're under when things go wrong. Tenants calling, deadlines looming, budgets tight. I get things done timely, fast, and efficiently because I know what it's like when you're depending on someone.

I'm very versatile and skilled - I can be your one go-to person to get most work done. And when something needs a bigger team or more specialized skills, I'll tell you honestly and help you find the right people.
