The Most Annoying Kitchen Problems

You know that kitchen cupboard door that won't close properly? The one that drives you crazy every single day? Or the drawer that sticks and fights you every time you try to open it?

I fix these problems fast. Same day service across Byron Bay, Ballina, Lismore, and Tweed Heads. Quality hardware, honest pricing, and work that actually lasts.

Door Won't Close

Cupboard doors that swing open or won't stay closed. Usually broken hinges or misaligned doors. Fixed with quality marine-grade hinges: $25 per hinge.

Drawer Sliders Broken

Drawers that stick, fall out, or won't close properly. Replace with soft-close slides that work smoothly for years: $45 per drawer.

Doors Hanging Wrong

Misaligned cupboard doors that look crooked or don't line up. Rehang properly so they look professional: $35 per door.

Handles Coming Off

Loose or broken handles and knobs. Install with proper backing and screws so they stay put: $15 per handle.

Shelves Sagging

Cabinet shelves that sag under weight. Reinforce or replace with stronger supports: $40 per shelf.

Complete Assessment

Full kitchen cabinet inspection and quote for all problems. Know exactly what needs fixing before we start: $80 (credited to work).

I understand Northern Rivers kitchens. The humidity from coastal air affects hinges and slides. Salt air corrodes cheap hardware. I use marine-grade hinges and quality slides that handle our environment.

From Byron Bay beachfront apartments to Lismore heritage homes - I've fixed thousands of kitchen problems. I carry quality hardware in my van so most jobs get finished the same day.
